Plomeek Soup

Description

A classic dish from a classic Star Trek race - Plomeek soup, a Vulcan dish. Most Vulcans, including the most famous one, Spock, are vegetarian. This is described as being quite a bland soup.

Ingredients

  • 1 onion
  • 2 garlic cloves
  • 5 carrots
  • 2 small-medium sweet potatoes, peeled
  • 3 small-medium beetroots, peeled
  • 3 sticks celery
  • 1 litre vegetable stock
  • Salt and pepper
  • Vegetable oil
  • Parsley for garnish (optional)

Rokeg Blood Pie

Description

So let’s get one thing straight. Rokeg Blood Pie, in my opinion, should be made with…blood. None of this raspberry puree stuff. I just can’t imagine a bunch of battle-weary Klingons sitting down to a delicate raspberry pie as part of a main meal. Blood, on the other hand, has all sorts of iron-enhancing properties which I am sure is good for preparing for battle.

Ingredients

  • 600ml / 20 fl oz pig’s blood
  • 150ml / 5 fl oz heavy cream
  • 280g / 10oz pork back fat, finely diced
  • 280g / 10oz onions, finely diced
  • 2 tablespoons rolled oats
  • 1 tablespoon salt
  • ½ tablespoon smoked paprika
  • ½ tablespoon sweet paprika
  • ½ tablespoon nutmeg
  • ½ tablespoon white pepper
  • ½ tablespoon chili flakes (optional)
  • 3 small beetroots with long roots (if preparing the pie to look like it does on The Next Generation)
  • 180g / 6 1/3oz butter, at room temperature
  • 240g / 81/2oz flour
  • 1-2 tablespoons ice water
